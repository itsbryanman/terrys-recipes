Title: Emeril Lagasse’s Rustic Rub Recipe

Emeril Lagasse’s Rustic Rub Recipe:

Combine all ingredients and store in an air-tight container.

Originally appeared in “Louisiana Real and Rustic” by Emeril Lagasse with Marcelle Bienvenu, 1996, William Morrow & Co., New York, NY, and is used here with permission.

Ingredients:
- 4 Tablespoon Paprika
- 1 1/2 Tablespoon Cayenne Pepper
- 2 1/2 Tablespoon Freshly Ground Black Pepper
- 3 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
- 1 1/2 Tablespoon Onion Powder
- 3 Tablespoon Salt
- 1 1/4 Tablespoon Dried Oregano
- 1 1/4 Tablespoon Dried Thyme

No directions found.
